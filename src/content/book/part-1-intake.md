Every piece of L&D work starts with a request landing on the team. Someone asks for a course. Someone asks for a workshop. Someone forwards a calendar invite. The way that request is handled — what's asked, what's deferred, what's qualified out — shapes everything downstream.

Not just how work comes in
Agencies have a name for this stage and a discipline around it. Most internal teams don't.

This part is about both: giving the stage a name, and giving it a discipline.
